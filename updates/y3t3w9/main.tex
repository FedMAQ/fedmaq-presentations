% Section 1: The Strategic Pivot
% ==========================================
\begin{frame}{Research Status}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item We shifted focus from physical IoT/Smart Campus energy forecasting to classical Federated Learning.
        \item We chose image classification (e.g., MNIST, CIFAR) to make experiments more manageable and reproducibility easier.
        \item We want to explore multi-adaptive quantization with server-side knowledge distillation with the hopes of further reducing communication while maintaining comparable accuracy.
    \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
